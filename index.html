Project Experience

May. 2012-Aug. 2012: The design of Microstrip Balance Mixer. The operating frequency is 9.41GHz and the LO frequency is 7.41GHz. The structure of 3dB directional coupler with the feature of the suppression of image frequency was adopted. The two signals with 90°phase difference enter through a Schottky diode with nonlinear characteristics and enter into IF ports. The Gain is -16.8dB and the NF is 18.1dB.
Aug. 2012-Oct. 2012: The design of S-band waveguide filter. The passband frequency is 2.02-2.08GHz and stopband frequency is 2.22GHz and 2.29GHz respectively. The structure of rectangular waveguide based on Chebyshev full pin match theory was adopted. By analysising the zero extraction of the circuit and synthesizing the circuit, the curve of ratio of impedance converter and pin diameter was obtained. The passband ripple less than 0.25dB and VSWR less than 1.2.
Apr. 2010-Jun. 2010: The simulation of evacuation in large public places based on artificial potential field. The project built the model of the procedure of evacuation in fire disaster and dynamically simulated it based on MFC. The project won the 2010 National Simulation Grand Prix Award.
